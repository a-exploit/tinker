Prof. Inderdeep Singh

Coordinator - Tinkering Lab
Associate Professor

The Tinkering lab has been conceptualized and developed in order to complement the engineering education with much-needed hands-on tinkering, giving wings to the spirit of creativity and innovation among students of IIT Roorkee. Tinkering Lab is a platform for creative minds to come out of their "Think Space" to hands on "Tinker Space", where they can transform their ideas into real-time engineering products.

Since its inception, the lab has provided active support to the projects from the diversified fields. A large number of students from almost all departments, years of study or academic programs and Students’ Technical Council have been benefited by the facilities available in the lab.

Tinkering Lab has already revolutionized the thought process of students towards the ‘Design-based Thinking Process’ and in future, the Tinkering Lab is expected to be a hub of innovation with focus on creative idea generation, prototyping and product development.
